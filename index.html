This whole block is an actual html div, nothing special about it.

The floating 3d objects however are rendered in two canvas elements - one for foreground and one for background.

They are rendered using three.js w/ two identical cameras, but w/ different frustums.

Bg camera frustum renders everything behind this block, and the fg camera frustum renders everything in front.

The effect makes it seem like an html div is immersed in a three.js scene, but actually the div is wrapped with two 3d renderers splitting the 3d world at the div plane.
